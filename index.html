About

Who we are:

Basically JYOT is a concept more than an organization…!

    Established on 5th September ’17, encouraged by the thought to uplift marginalized commonality and mitigate the darkness hindering its development by innovative ideas and thereby providing them a small JYOT- enough to give rays of hope to the lives.


Vision

  • Provide financial assistance and implement skill-based initiatives for all-round development of students.
  • Create a mutually strong generation by overcoming depression.
  • Foster the bond of affection between educated youth and underprivileged.
  • Integrate traditions and culture with novel ideas for social upliftment.





Mission

  • Reaching underprivileged to aid is our aim.

  • "Problem once faced, shouldn't be faced twice!" To accomplish this, our prime focus is providing indigents the guidance from experienced people who have already invested in such commonly encountered issues and have managed to find a way out..!

  • By working on wreck 'JYOT' family owns the strength to create eternal solutions for these objectives.

    • History & Work


        We believe while leading a life as a 'social animal', we must go beyond our basic needs of food, shelter and clothing and fulfill the duty of giving back to society as a whole. We carry in our hearts a solicitation to pave a way towards the upliftment of the poor, orphan and downtrodden members of the world in which we live. Out of this urge to aid the needy, we have established “JYOT”-to enlighten the lives of the beings who were till today far from the reach of light of a purposeful life. Read more...

    200+ Volunteers:

        Human beings have been interacting and socializing with one another since ancient times. The importance of foundation to people’s social existence cannot be denied. Social Foundations help humans to achieve some of the five human necessities as described by Abraham Maslow.
      People have a need to socialize with others with whom they share a common interest. Taking the above things into consideration, JYOT was founded. The foundation was laid by dedicated and passionate young social activists of Government College of Engineering, Amravati for making a difference in society especially for the betterment of the underprivileged by initiating a number of influencing drives at the college level. As the activities started reaching out to people and getting appreciation for the efforts made, more and more enthusiastic and selfless youths came forward to bestow themselves in the work. With the efforts of all volunteers, the Jyot family keeps growing and diversifying its roots towards progress.
      For the betterment of the downtrodden & underprivileged, now, JYOT has been enlightening all over Maharashtra with 200+ working volunteers.

    More Than 20+ Initiatives

        “Things may come to those who wait, but only the things left by those who hustle.” Thus it is very necessary to take initiative whenever opportunity knocks on the door. An Initiative is to success as a lighted match is to a candle.
     JYOT is that light and we make efforts to take every possible initiative which we believe can bring a change in society. We have taken 20 + initiatives that had massive impacts giving us strength and inspired good actions in others.
     These initiatives include trees plantation drive, felicitation of guards, organization of various social seminars, and so on. We have done various activities right from donation of Notes to donation of Clothes. The unique and distinctive way of JYOT’S ShivJayanti, Diwali, Rakshabandhan, and National festivals are a few of our most recognized initiatives.
     These initiatives are little steps of success that are paving our path to the dream destination of JYOT – a society with adequate support for all those underprivileged who want to raise their standards level high but are drawn back by their situation. We are on a mission to bring about huge changes through our small but powerful initiatives. We are right here planning for better Tomorrow starting Today and for that, we will further make sure to keep taking such initiatives as :
    “A good plan implemented today is better than a perfect plan implemented tomorrow.”

    Working for 15+ Schools and Villages:

        As truly said by Gandhiji “Future of India lies in villages” , our villages are the reservoir of immense potential especially for the young generation. India relied heavily on industrialization and the building of material prosperity and villages remain primitive. Education, health, infrastructure, sanitation are some sectors that are still underdeveloped.
      We are working in 15+ schools and villages to make some meaningful change in the future of India. For the bright future, the make or break factor is education. We teach students for various competitive exams like the Scholarship exam, Navoday to make them capable for today’s era of competition.
      Another sector that needs more attention is sanitation. We arrange a cleanliness campaign and spread awareness about diseases caused due to poor hygiene. This initiative has gained much significance in the Covid-19 pandemic. We are carrying out the door-to-door survey, awareness about precautionary measures, and vaccination.
      While making great strides towards achieving $5 trillion we can’t ignore proficient villages because:
      India’s way is not Europe’s. India is not Calcutta and Bombay. India lives in her seven hundred thousand villages.

    Work

    What we do:

    An Eternal Treasure

      Old-age is another childhood and thus it is our civic duty to take care of our elderly providing them with the care and respect they deserve as they have so much to guide us. ... Their warmth, comfort, elegance, and golden wisdom touched our souls. Every wrinkle on their face shows their struggles throughout life, their wisdom-highlights symbolize numerous sweet and bitter experiences. In the fast life, we don’t have time to even turn the page of the calendar but they are counting each day to do so.
      With our visit to an old age home we discovered "the best classroom in the world is in the feet of an elderly person".

    Study Material Distribution: A Gift That Never Expires!

      The notes distribution program of JYOT is the epitome of this aspiration of Mahatma Jyotiba Phule. Everyone, especially those with some financial problem struggles for notes and study materials in the first year of their college. ...
    So JYOT started one innovative initiative called the ‘Notes Distribution Program’. In this program, notes were collected from 2nd, 3rd-year students and distributed to needy students of the 1st year. The concept began in the year 2017 when few members of JYOT recognized the difficulty faced by students in buying academic books and relevant notes of curriculum and on the other hand many books after use are given away to scrap dealers.   True education signifies empowering others and leaving a world behind better than the one we found!

    Shiv Jayanti: An Unending Era of Magnificence

      JYOT decided to celebrate the Jayanti of the great warrior with an unique idea. To raise funds for the celebration of Shiv Jayanti, we organized an aptitude test with nominal fees of 10 rupees. With this initiative, a fund of 2000 rupees have been raised. ...
     We again came up with an amazing idea of the "Chaha-Poha stall”. This event was a great success where students experienced a different spirit, fun and connection. Apart from the joy an ample amount of ₹6000 was raised.
      The JYOT Foundation kept spreading the happiness cycle by celebrating the day with Orphan children at Sada Shanti Ashram Amravati. Here the amount was utilized for getting a one-month ration and Solar lamps for children, the concept of glee and gaiety to children while giving immense satisfaction to JYOT members. In this way we made a deep obeisance not only to the brave warrior but also to the era of unending glory.

    Planting Hope

      JYOT believes, to snooze the warning alarm of nature, the simple and obvious solution is Tree Plantation only. To make it possible for the coming generations we aspired to put in our little efforts. ...
     To see the mother earth at its best, we have been coming forward with various tree plantation drives every year with the mantra "Plant a sapling, Fill the space and Heal the earth."
     We aim to plant more and more trees to ensure a green Earth, fertile soil, rich flora- fauna, diverse biodiversity, a balanced environment, and a happy human.
     It is said, “He who plants a tree. Plants a hope”. Team Jyot implanted the same hope to ensure a safe and secure generation of the successors.

    COVID Free Maharashtra

      Indulging our efforts for making betterment in the current scenario, members of JYOT stepped onto the grounds to make our society free from the sufferings of the Covid-19 pandemic. ...
     Keeping in track, the information about all aspects of growth in no of Covid cases, increasing fatality rate & checking it’s spread through the carriers has been the action plan mapped by the entire team.
     Taking into consideration all issues JYOT came up with an initiative of “ COVID free Maharashtra" The first step included door to door survey, creating an awareness in the rural areas about the disease, precautionary measures in the daily life schedules, and self-health care routines while in-home quarantine, vaccination, etc. Surveys have been carried by them recently in Dhamangaon and Akot where we visited the rural areas and collected data regarding the same.
     Team JYOT has been taking all the precautions. while carrying on the survey. We have just stepped into the grounds, victory is yet to be achieved.

    Fiber of Ambition

      Adding warmth and happiness to the lives, JYOT came up with an initiative of CLOTH DONATION. Members collected used and old clothes from the college students and donated them to little kids of the Melghat region. ....
     Seeing their smiling faces after receiving that clothes, our hearts had an essence of realization about the irony our society is living with!
     Privileged kids fuss about getting short of clothes that don’t fit for a particular list of occasions whilst some unfortunate children don't have one pair too!
     What we realized after doing this act was, it felt as if we made those beautiful souls a part of our lives by making them fit into those pieces of clothes we lived into someday!
     Having such experience can make you count the graciousness GOD has gifted you with! Team JYOT feels grateful to have that feeling!

    F.A.Q

    Frequently Asked Questions

    • 1.What is the vision of JYOT?

      Answer: Our Vision is to bridge the gap between the educated youth and the underprivileged. We are on a mission to bring about big changes through our small but powerful initiatives. We are up to giving a better Tomorrow starting Today. If our efforts can make little positive change in society then they are justified!

    • 2. Who can join JYOT?

      Answer: Anybody who believes in our vision and has the same perspective to help others in need is more than welcome to be a part of Jyot. One can volunteer in our drives and contribute whatever one wishes through the organization.

    • 3. How can I join JYOT?

      Answer: Please go to our "Contact Us" section and contact our team. Also you can below founders and team members, you are always welcome to contribute with JYOT.

    • 4.What are various Initiatives that JYOT is involved with?

      Answer: We have been actively working on new and creative social drives with every year's blooming team of JYOT. We have successfully completed notes and study material donation drive, clothes distribution, educating children at the orphanage, etc. You can go on through the work section and history page to know more.

    • 5.How can I help other than making a financial contribution? What all can I give/contribute?

      Answer: It is not mandatory for anyone at JYOT to contribute financially. You can also give your services to society through JYOT by teaching children at the orphanage, donating old notes or clothes, helping out in small chores during various events organized by us. Also, your time and efforts are always welcomed.
      But if you want to contribute financially then you can go to Donate section where you can donate as TOGETHER WE CAN IMPROVE THEIR LIVES!

    • 6. Who runs the organization?

      Answer: JYOT depends upon its various teams consisting of various students from different paths of life. These students overall as an organization run JYOT.
